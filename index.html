Welcome to Memphis Hack Club!


We meet every Friday at 4:00 PM CST on zoom.memphishack.com




Why Come?


• Workshops are beginner friendly!


• MHC provides a great learning enviorment!


• Many opportunities to further advance coding skils!


• Make great friends in our open & inclusive community!




How to get into the main Discord Channels


• Come to a zoom meeting with your camera turned on.


• If you were invited by a friend or teacher message Philip on discord @philip or @Philip's Mac.




Support Memphis Hack Club


No member of Memphis Hack Club is required or should feel obligated to donate

or buy merchandise, but as a nonprofit organization it greatly helps make Memphis

Hack Club better! Monetary support is never expected, but always greatly apreciated!
